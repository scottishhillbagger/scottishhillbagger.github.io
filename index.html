A field guide to

Scottish Hill Bagging

The Hills
Munros282
Mountains over 3,000ft (914m), first catalogued by Sir Hugh Munro in his 1891 Tables. The list is maintained by the Scottish Mountaineering Club.
Corbetts222
Hills between 2,500–3,000ft (762–914m) with at least 500ft (152m) of prominence. Named for John Rooke Corbett, an early Scottish Mountaineering Club member.
Fionas219
Hills between 2,000–2,500ft (610–762m) with at least 500ft (152m) of prominence. Formerly known as Grahams.
Bothies86
Simple shelters in remote country, free to use, maintained by the Mountain Bothies Association.
